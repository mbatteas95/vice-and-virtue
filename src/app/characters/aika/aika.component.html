KONEKO AIKA

NAME: 子猫 (の)哀歌 – AIKA KONEKO
AGE | BIRTHDAY: 24 YEARS OLD | MAY 15TH (TAURUS)
HEIGHT | WEIGHT: 158 CM (5’2”) | 44 KG (97 LBS)
HAIR: BLACK
EYES: SOFT BLUE
OCCUPATION: ASPIRING ARTIST, PART-TIME BARISTA
VICES: ANYTHING THAT'LL TAKE THE EDGE OFF OF A HARD DAY


◆ SARCASTIC, MELLOW, PESSIMISTIC REALIST, POSSIBLY FLIRTY AFTER A DRINK OR TWO. (IF YOU'RE BUYIN'.)
◆ COME FOR THE WITTY REMARKS AND SASS, STAY FOR A GOOD CONVERSATION.
◆ RENTS THE SMALL FLAT JUST ABOVE THE COFFEE SHOP WHERE SHE WORKS. THE PLACE DOUBLES AS HER "STUDIO" AND LIVING SPACE.
◆ SINCE SHE HAS YET TO MAKE HER "BIG BREAK" WITH HER ARTWORK, AIKA SUPPLEMENTS HER INCOME THROUGH VARIOUS OTHER UNDERHANDED MEANS. WATCH YOUR POCKETS AND YOUR WITS.
◆ PREFERS OIL PAINTS, BUT WORKS WITH SEVERAL DIFFERENT MEDIUMS.
◆ TIT FOR TAT: DEALS IN FAVORS (EASILY WON OVER BY SWEETS, COFFEE, OR FREE FOOD/DRINKS)
